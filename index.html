Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our WhatsApp Business service, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Your phone number, name, and WhatsApp profile information
  • Pet Information: Details about your pet including species, name, weight, and health concerns
  • Location Data: Your address to determine service availability and arrange home visits
  • Communication Records: Messages exchanged through our WhatsApp Business service
  • Appointment Information: Scheduling details and service preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing veterinary consultations and home visit services
  • Scheduling and confirming appointments
  • Communicating about your pet's health and treatment
  • Processing payments for services
  • Improving our services and customer experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Licensed Veterinarians: Information necessary for providing medical care to your pet
  • Emergency Situations: When required to protect the health and safety of your pet
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our business (payment processors, etc.)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure communication channels through WhatsApp Business
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure storage of physical and digital records

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Active Communication: While you are an active client
  • Medical Records: As required by veterinary regulations (typically 3-7 years)
  • Inactive Accounts: 2 years after last contact, unless legally required to retain longer

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing your personal information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for processing (where consent is the legal basis)

7. WhatsApp Business Specific Information

Our WhatsApp Business service is subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policies:

  • Messages are encrypted end-to-end
  • WhatsApp may store messages temporarily for delivery
  • We use WhatsApp Business API for automated responses
  • Message metadata may be processed for service improvement

8. Cookies and Tracking

This website may use cookies to improve user experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for pet owners who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside of Israel where our service providers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this website and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through our WhatsApp Business service during business hours.
